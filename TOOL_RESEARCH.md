## SPOTIO

### Overview
SPOTIO is the leading field sales engagement platform built for high-volume D2D and canvassing teams. Primary use case: territory-based door-to-door sales, field team coordination, and pipeline management. Target: B2B and B2C field sales teams with 5+ reps in roofing, solar, home improvement, telecom, and enterprise sales.

### Pricing (2025)
- **Team:** $25/user/month (annual) | $32/user/month (quarterly)
- **Business:** $49/user/month (annual) | $59/user/month (monthly)
- **Pro:** $129/user/month
- **Enterprise:** Custom
- **Minimum:** 5 users required
- **Free Trial:** None — demo/pilot only
- **Billing:** Annual, quarterly, or monthly options

### Ratings
- **G2:** 4.5/5 — 387 reviews | Data Visualization: 9.7/10 | Task Management: 9.0/10 | Won 31 G2 awards Winter 2024
- **Capterra:** 4.3/5 — 78 reviews

### Company
- **Founded:** 2014 | **HQ:** Addison, TX
- **Funding:** $4.75M total (Ballast Point Ventures, CreativeCo Capital, Lighter Capital)
- **Revenue:** $3.6M (2024, up from $2.4M in 2023)
- **Users:** 85,000+ sales professionals | 1,500+ teams

### Top Praised
- Best-in-class territory mapping (200+ data overlay points)
- Deep analytics and rep performance dashboards
- Strong integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, AccuLynx, Zapier 5,000+ apps)
- No. 1 rated D2D app on App Store and Google Play
- Multi-channel activity tracking; data visualization scored 9.7/10

### Top Complaints
- App crashes and random logouts
- Call connection problems (flagged as spam)
- Mapping pin glitches
- Some users report poor customer support + cancellation billing issues
- 5-user minimum blocks solo/small teams
- Reporting is activity-focused, not revenue-focused

### Best For
Teams of 10+ in solar, telecom, roofing, home security. Mid-market to enterprise needing sophisticated territory management and analytics.

### Not For
Solo reps or sub-5-user teams. Simple canvassing-only ops. Those needing revenue-centric reporting or SOC 2 compliance.

### Key Differentiators
1. Territory mapping excellence (200+ data point overlays, parent/child structures)
2. Data visualization: 9.7/10 on G2 — best in category
3. Broadest integration ecosystem (5,000+ via Zapier + native Salesforce, HubSpot, NetSuite, AccuLynx)
4. SPOTIO AI launched March 2025 — instant insights, coaching tools, messaging templates
5. B2B focus depth vs. competitors' B2C lean

### Recent Updates (2024–2025)
- **March 2025:** SPOTIO AI — instant selling insights, messaging templates, manager coaching
- **2024:** Activity type classification (In-Person vs. Virtual), task management refinements
- 31 G2 awards Winter 2024

### Notable Customers

### Integrations
Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, Oracle NetSuite, Pipedrive, Zoho, AccuLynx, JobNimbus, Zapier (5,000+), Open API

## SALESRABBIT

### Overview
SalesRabbit is an all-in-one field sales management platform built specifically for D2D. Provides lead management, territory mapping, appointment scheduling, digital contracts, and team coordination. Strongest in roofing and solar verticals. Just acquired Roofle (Jan 2026) to build unified roofing platform.

### Pricing (2025)
- **Lite:** $0/month — 1 user, free forever (leads deleted after 90 days if not modified)
- **Team:** $195/month for 5 seats (~$39/user) annual | higher quarterly/monthly | $399 setup fee
- **Pro:** $245/month for 5 seats (~$49/user) annual | includes API access, forms, digital contracts | $399 setup fee
- **Enterprise:** Custom (100+ reps) — SSO, coaching tools, dedicated support
- **Free Trial:** Lite plan is permanent free tier (1 user, 3 devices max)

### Ratings
- **G2:** 4.5/5 — 426 reviews | #1 Small-Business Results Index for Field Sales | #2 Enterprise Grid Report | 39 awards Fall 2023, 31 Spring 2024
- **Capterra:** 4.2/5 — 193 reviews

### Company
- **Founded:** 2013 | **HQ:** Lehi, UT
- **Funding:** $4.27M (Kickstart Seed Fund, Diversis Capital, Album VC)
- **Revenue:** $5M (2024) — outpacing SPOTIO despite similar funding
- **Employees:** ~108–124 (slight decline 2024→2025)
- **Users:** 85,000+ active sales professionals

### Top Praised
- Intuitive mobile-first interface — reps learn quickly
- Robust offline mode — works in dead zones
- 98% customer support satisfaction score
- Easy territory assignment and lead organization
- DataGrid AI for lead prioritization
- Appointment scheduling and digital contracts built-in

### Top Complaints

- No SOC 2 Type 2 — procurement blocker for some enterprise buyers
- Monthly billing is 67% more expensive than annual

### Best For
Roofing (primary), solar, telecom, pest control, home security. Mobile-first teams 5–500 reps who value ease of use and strong support.

### Not For
Enterprise requiring SOC 2 compliance. Teams needing heavy CRM customization or revenue-centric reporting.

### Key Differentiators
1. **Roofle acquisition (Jan 2026)** — adds e-commerce pricing, AI financing, digital proposals; building complete roofing tech stack (field + online + production)
2. Mobile-first architecture with proven offline mode
3. Free forever Lite plan — lowest barrier to entry in category
4. 98% support satisfaction score — marketable differentiator
5. Roofing vertical depth: RoofLink (CRM) + Roofle (e-commerce) + SalesRabbit (field)
6. DataGrid AI for pre-canvass lead scoring

### Recent Updates (2024–2025)

### Overview
Badger Maps is the leading route optimization and mobile mapping platform for outside sales reps. Lets salespeople visualize customers on maps, plan optimized routes (up to 120 stops), manage territories, and log check-ins. Built for account managers visiting existing clients, not pure D2D canvassing.

### Pricing (2025)
- **Business:** $49/user/month (annual) | $59/user/month (monthly)
- **Enterprise:** $95/user/month (annual) | $105/user/month (monthly)
- **Free Trial:** 14-day individual trial; pilot programs for teams of 2+
- **Minimum:** No published minimum
- **Guarantee:** 90-day money-back, no questions asked
- **Setup Fees:** $0

### Ratings
- **G2:** 4.7/5 — 270 reviews | Ease of Use: 9.2 | Support: 9.4 | #1 route optimization category
- **Capterra:** 4.6/5 — 199–203 reviews

### Company
- **Founded:** 2012 (most established in this list) | **HQ:** San Francisco, CA
- **Funding:** $490K (Alchemist Accelerator, StartX, Lighter Capital, PPP)
- **Revenue:** $8M (2025), $7.7M (2024) — profitable, capital-efficient
- **Customers:** ~4,000 | **Valuation:** ~$18.5M (2025)

### Top Praised
- "Lasso" feature: circle customers on map → instant optimized route
- Drastically reduces planning time (3–4 hours → 15 minutes for weekly route)
- Users report fitting 2 more meetings per day
- Excellent support (9.4/10 on G2 — highest in category)
- No setup/integration fees — transparent pricing
- 90-day money-back guarantee
- Two-way real-time CRM sync

### Top Complaints
- Mobile app crashes and freezing
- Location inaccuracy issues (pins wrong position)
- Notes disappear on occasion
- Limited offline functionality
- Settings and filters confusing for new users
- No rep-to-rep or rep-to-customer messaging

### Best For
Field sales reps visiting existing accounts: pharmaceutical, food & bev, home services, real estate, pest control. Teams of 1–300 who need route efficiency above all else.

### Not For
Pure D2D canvassing teams (SPOTIO/SalesRabbit better). Offline-heavy operations. Teams needing built-in communication tools.

### Key Differentiators
1. "Lasso" route builder — unique UX not found in competitors
2. Most established company in this category (founded 2012, 4,000+ customers)
3. Highest G2 support score (9.4) — "team actually answers the phone"
4. 90-day money-back guarantee — removes purchase risk
5. Proven ROI claims: "20% less driving, 22% more sales"
6. Profitable bootstrapped company ($8M revenue)

### Recent Updates (2024–2025)
- Collapsible filters in web app
- Route assignment (assign routes to specific team members)
- Ongoing CRM integration improvements (Salesforce, HubSpot, Dynamics, Zoho, Pipedrive, NetSuite)

- Enterprise-grade territory management
- Works with existing Salesforce workflows, reports, and dashboards
